我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

  • 定位信息：仅在你使用 App 时，用于搜索附近厕所、计算路线和显示方向。
  • 评价数据：如果你提交评价，App 会保存评分以及标签，例如干净程度、是否开放、是否免费、楼层提示。
  • 设备传感器数据：App 可能使用方向、指南针和气压计数据来辅助箭头方向和上下楼提示。这些数据用于本机计算。
  • Location: used while the app is in use to find nearby restrooms, calculate routes, and show direction guidance.
  • Review data: if you submit a review, the app stores rating and tags such as cleanliness, availability, cost, and floor hints.
  • Device sensor data: heading, compass, and barometer data may be used locally to improve arrow direction and floor hints.

我们如何使用信息 / How We Use Information

定位信息用于通过 Apple MapKit 搜索附近厕所和计算步行路线。评价标签用于帮助你和其他用户判断当前厕所是否适合使用。

Location is used with Apple MapKit to search nearby restroom points of interest and calculate walking routes. Review tags help you and other users understand restroom conditions.

数据存储与共享 / Storage and Sharing

评价数据通过 Apple CloudKit Public Database 保存。App 不要求创建账号，不上传图片，不保存自由文字评论，不销售个人信息。

Review data is stored through Apple CloudKit Public Database. The app does not require account creation, upload photos, store free-text comments, or sell personal information.

第三方服务 / Third-Party Services

App 使用 Apple MapKit 提供地点搜索和路线能力，并使用 Apple CloudKit 保存轻量评价数据。这些服务受 Apple 的相关条款和隐私政策约束。

The app uses Apple MapKit for place search and routing, and Apple CloudKit for lightweight review data. These services are governed by Apple's applicable terms and privacy policies.

儿童隐私 / Children’s Privacy

App 面向普通用户，不包含面向儿童的社交功能、自由评论区或广告追踪。

The app is intended for general audiences and does not include child-directed social features, free-text comment areas, or advertising tracking.

你的选择 / Your Choices

你可以在 iOS 设置中关闭定位权限。关闭定位后，App 将无法自动搜索附近厕所或提供实时方向。你也可以选择不提交评价。

You can disable Location permission in iOS Settings. Without Location permission, the app cannot automatically find nearby restrooms or provide live direction guidance. You may also choose not to submit reviews.
